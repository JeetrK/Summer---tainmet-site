JPEGMAFIA (Barrington DeVaughn Hendricks, "Peggy") is a multi-talented experimental rapper, producer, performer and leader in the underground rap scene. After producing six tracks on Kanye West’s latest album and a very successful 2023 with “SCARING!” JPEGMAFIA returns with a new album and worldwide headline tour titled I AM PEGGY! This run follows his very successful co-headline tour alongside Danny Brown for their collaborative album “SCARING!” which included sold-out shows nationwide including landmark venues like The Hollywood Palladium. The album debuted at number 84 on the Billboard 200 and entered at position 39 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart, while also charting in six other countries including Canada and the UK. In recent years, JPEGMAFIA has released critically acclaimed projects such as "Veteran" (2018), "All My Heroes Are Cornballs" (2019), and "LP!" (2021) – with praise from The Guardian, XXL, Office Mag, Complex, and more.
